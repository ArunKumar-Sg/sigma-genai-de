GOLD LAYER	[ Table name, key columns, EXACT business rule formula ]	
→ Your answer	"Table Name: marketing_attribution

Join Condition
transactions.promo_id = promotions.promo_id

Business Rules
promo_cost = transaction_amount * (discount_pct / 100)

revenue_per_channel = SUM(transaction_amount) GROUP BY channel

promo_cost_per_channel = SUM(promo_cost) GROUP BY channel

roi_per_channel = (revenue_per_channel - promo_cost_per_channel) / promo_cost_per_channel

Final Columns

channel
revenue_per_channel
promo_cost_per_channel
roi_per_channel
run_date"
